Route Optimization

Route optimization is a process of planning and creating the shortest delivery path to reduce turnaround time for your drivers.

Once you have downloaded the “Route Optimization” plugin, it will appear in your main Odoo dashboard.

How Route Optimization Works

Route optimization is the process of determining the most efficient route to deliver goods or services. This process considers factors such as traffic conditions, vehicle capacity, delivery time windows, and customer locations.

  • When you open the app, you can see the list of your delivery addresses.
  • Once a new order is received from the Odoo Sales Module.
  • This delivery address gets updated on the delivery list in “the Odoo Route Optimization Module.” You can also see the address marked on the delivery map by clicking over the location icon in the extreme right corner.
  • Click on “Optimization”
  • With a single click, the delivery addresses are sequenced and arranged in which address to deliver first to reduce delivery time.
  • Once the route is successfully optimized, the delivery list is rearranged accordingly.
  • You can also refer to the arrangement of these addresses on the delivery map. It shows numbering on the map guides, which address to deliver first.

How to Configure:

  • Step 1: Check if partner's geolocation module is installed, if not, first install it, if yes, then Go to Contacts app and click on partner assignment tab and then compute latitude and longitude of partner's addresses.
  • Step 2: Go to Fleet app
  • Step 3: Add a vehicle
  • Step 4: Add Longitude and Latitude on Location field in "Add Vehicle" form
  • Step 5: Move that Vehicle to Registered tab in a Kanban view
  • Step 6: Now Go to Route optimization App and See the sales orders
  • Step 7: Now Click on "Optimization", it will optimize your routes to save time, fuel and increase efficiency.

To Integrate Google Maps:

  • Step 1: Go to settings.
  • Step 2: Search for "Geolocation"
  • Step 3: Check the checkbox of Geolocation to activate it.
  • Step 4: Click On "Save".
  • Step 5: Choose "Google Place Map"
  • Step 6: Enter Google key
  • Step 7: Click On "Save".
